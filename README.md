# sales-data-analysis-sql
SQL project analyzing sales performance using revenue trends, product analysis, and window functions to generate business insights.


##  Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance using SQL to extract meaningful business insights from transactional sales data.  
The goal is to evaluate revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior to support data-driven decision-making.

##  Objectives
- Calculate total and monthly revenue
- Identify best-selling products and categories

##  Key Insights

###  Overall Performance
- **Total revenue** provides a baseline measure of overall business performance.
- **Average order value** reflects typical customer spending per order.

###  Monthly Sales Analysis
- Monthly revenue shows a **declining trend from January to May**.
- **January** is the highest revenue-generating month, while **May** ranks the lowest.
- Revenue ranking confirms the downward sales trend over time.

###  Category Performance
- High-volume categories such as **Stationery, Clothing, and Home & Kitchen** dominate unit sales.
- These categories indicate strong demand for everyday-use products.

###  Product Performance
- **Wireless Mouse** is the most frequently purchased product, indicating consistent customer demand for essential electronic accessories.

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Investigate factors contributing to higher revenue in January and declining performance in later months.
- Focus promotions and inventory planning on high-demand, frequently purchased products.
- Combine sales volume and revenue analysis to identify both popular and profitable categories.
